About me

I am a dedicated software engineer currently honing my skills at VolPTech Solutions, DLF Cybercity, Infocity, Bhubaneswar, Odisha, where I work on critical telecommunications and cloud infrastructure. My daily work involves hands-on experience with AWS cloud services, Linux environments, SQL, Putty troubleshooting, and VoIP platforms like VOOS 3000, Veriswitch, VTS Dialler, VICI Dial and Fusion PBX , providing me with a strong foundation in backend systems and real-world troubleshooting.

Alongside managing SMS (Promotional, Transational and OTP) service integrations in various platforms and working with Direct Inward Dialing (DID) and Toll-Free Number (TFN) configurations.

I am a collaborative team player with experience in leadership, seeking to apply my problem-solving skills to create impactful software solutions.
